How to Play

  1. Click to Shoot: Shoot projectiles by clicking anywhere on the screen. Projectiles fly toward the cursor.
  2. Avoid Enemies: Enemies spawn from the edges and move toward you. If they touch you, it's game over!
  3. Destroy Enemies: Hit enemies with projectiles. Bigger ones shrink first before exploding.
  4. Score Points: You get 50 points for shrinking and 100 points for destroying an enemy.
  5. Cool Explosions: Destroyed enemies create particle effects. No worries, they're harmless and purely decorative!
  6. Restart: Click the "Start Game" button after a game over to play again.
